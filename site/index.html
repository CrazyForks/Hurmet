Portal Frame

The nearby sketch shows a portal frame with a single lateral point load. With only that information, one can draw the general shape of the reactions, the shear diagram, the moment diagram, and the deflection diagram.

To get numerical detail, engineers these days are likely to use a frame analysis program, but a simple hand analysis will also do the job.
