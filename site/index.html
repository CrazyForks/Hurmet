John D. Cook recently posted a calculation to estimate the time to SARS-COV-2 herd immunity. (https://www.johndcook.com/blog/2021/02/19/herd-immunity-countdown/) He wrote his example in Python. Let’s see how it looks in Hurmet.

“Herd immunity” does not mean absolutely zero existence of COVID. It means that infection rates become low enough to allow life to get back to something like normal.

Many of these numbers are estimates, some with wide ranges. Feel free to plug in your own estimated values and check sensitivity to changes.
